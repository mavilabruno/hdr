Private Youtube Video

The module prevents the user from accessing the information and properties of the video on YouTube by blocking the links.

  1. Prevent sharing the YouTube video link.
  2. Prevents clicking on the video to redirect us to YouTube.
  3. SIt prevents subscribing to the YouTube channel.
  4. Prevent a new tab from opening in the browser when we click on the youtube logo.
